About us

Space 2 is an award winning arts and social change charity based in east Leeds. We are a small organisation that punches above our weight, with co-production principles at the heart of what we do. We have a passion for the arts and the social and personal outcomes it inspires. We create conditions through our projects that generate creativity and aspire to collaborate with our communities to enable us all to thrive.
